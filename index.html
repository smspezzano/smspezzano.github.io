About Me

   I recently changed the direction of my career path and after completing a web development program am now a full stack web developer.

    My specialties include Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. I also have some experience with Mustache.js, Backbone.js, AngularJS, and PostgreSQL.

   In addition to my coding skills I have several years of experience working in the business development department of a start-up. There I was tasked with data management and analysis. I also managed a team of admins. That experience left me with the ability to develop long term relationships with clients and help them reach their goals.

   Outside of work I am an avid San Francisco Bay Area sports fan. I also enjoy volunteering at the San Francisco Senior Center at Aquatic Park.
