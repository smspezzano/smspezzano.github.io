About Me

I recently changed the direction of my career path and after completing a web development program am now a full stack web developer. My specialties include Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. I also have some experience with Mustache.js, Backbone.js, and Postgress.

In addition to my coding skills I have several years of experience working in the business development department of a start-up. There I was tasked with data management and analysis and I also managed a small team of admins. That experience left me with the ability to develop long term relationships with clients and help them reach their goals.
